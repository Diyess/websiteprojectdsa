Preorder

(ROOT - LEFT - RIGHT)



Preorder traversal is a computer science algorithm that visits the nodes of a tree in a specific order, starting at the root node and then recursively traversing the left and right subtrees:
Visit the root node
Recursively traverse the left subtree
Recursively traverse the right subtree
Preorder traversal is also known as depth-first search. It's used to create copies of trees and to represent expression trees in prefix notation.
